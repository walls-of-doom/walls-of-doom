About


Walls of Doom is a minimalistic platformer written in ANSI C by Bernardo
Sulzbach and others.


The game is released under the BSD 3-Clause license for anyone to be able to
play and modify it.


The player gets points for surviving in a space enclosed by deadly walls with
constantly moving platforms where it may get perks that might help it survive
longer and also exchange points for extra lives.


Commands


  Arrows or Keypad        - Change running direction

  Space                   - Jump

  C                       - Convert points into a life

  I                       - Invest points

  P                       - Pause the game

  Q                       - Quit
